# Showcases

ANIP includes three showcase domains:

- travel
- finance
- DevOps

## Why they matter

These are not cosmetic examples.

They exercise:

- delegation and scope
- side-effect declarations
- cost signaling
- failures and recovery
- audit posture
- Studio integration

## What they are for

The showcase apps are best understood as:

- runnable reference applications
- evaluation surfaces
- realistic examples for docs and demos

They are not yet packaged as polished end-user products.
